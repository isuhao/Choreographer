About Choreographer

Choreographer is a sequencer / composition environment for spatial music. It is aimed at composers or sound artists seeking a flexible and intuitive tool for 3D sound source placement.

The audio rendering engine which is integrated in this software, is designed for sound spatialisation in Ambisonics and implements Ambisonics Equivalent Panning, a technique conceived at the ICST.
