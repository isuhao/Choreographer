Member Data Documentation

bool aepChannelSettings::enableMeasurement

Enables the measurement of the samples that are sent to the audio driver.

The measured values are measuredDecayingValue and measuredPeakValue.

float aepChannelSettings::measuredDecayingValue

If enableOutputMeasurement is set, the root mean square with a fall off is stored in this variable.

Measured are the samples that are sent to the audio driver.

float aepChannelSettings::measuredPeakValue

If enableOutputMeasurement is set, the peak value is stored in this variable.

Measured are the samples that are sent to the audio driver.

bool aepChannelSettings::solo

Engages the solo.
