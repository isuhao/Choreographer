bool aepChannelSettings::enableOutputMeasurement

Enables the measurement of the samples that are sent to the audio driver.

The measured values are measuredRootMeanSquareValue and measuredPeakValue.

double aepChannelSettings::measuredPeakValue

If enableOutputMeasurement is set, the peak value is stored in this variable.

Measured are the samples that are sent to the audio driver.

double aepChannelSettings::measuredRootMeanSquareValue
